Bontty allows you to scan through video mentions to cosmetic products from your favorite influencers as you browse Ulta and Sephora. Quickly see what they say and do to help you decide.

Install Bontty

Browse a product on Ulta or Sephora
Look at products as you normally would. Use the search box to find a specific one, or navigate through the different sections on Ulta or Sephora.

Check for the colored icon
When browsing a product, check Bontty's icon at the top right corner of Chrome. It will be colored if we found mentions for that product and grayed out otherwise. Go to the Product Mentions section to start looking at videos.

Scan through videos
Navigate videos from your favorite influencers to see how the product looks on skin, hear their opinions and discover similar products.
